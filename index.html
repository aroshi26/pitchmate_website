FAQs

What is PitchMate?

PitchMate is a cricket club management system designed to enhance player performance tracking, coaching efficiency, and club operations. It integrates web and mobile applications for structured feedback, team selection, and data-driven decision-making.

Who can use PitchMate?

PitchMate is designed for cricket club administrators, coaches, supporting staff (physios, doctors), and players. Administrators manage operations, coaches provide structured feedback, and players access performance insights through a mobile app.

Why is PitchMate necessary for cricket clubs?

Many clubs rely on outdated, manual record-keeping, leading to inefficiencies in tracking player performance, coaching effectiveness, and team selection. PitchMate digitizes and automates these processes, ensuring transparency and accuracy.

What are the key features of PitchMate?

PitchMate streamlines cricket club management with player performance tracking, structured coaching feedback, and automated rankings for batting, bowling, and all-rounders. It enhances team selection using AI recommendations, integrates medical records for player health tracking, and provides performance dashboards for insights. Secure authentication and role-based access ensure data protection and controlled access.

Is PitchMate accessible on mobile devices?

Yes, PitchMate provides a mobile application for players and a responsive web interface for administrators and coaches.
